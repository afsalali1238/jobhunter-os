How to Use JobHunter OS

Welcome to JobHunter OS. This isn't a normal web app—it's a system powered by your local AI code editor (like Cursor, Claude Code, or Antigravity). You don't click buttons to find jobs; you talk to your AI.

Step 1: The Initial Setup

To get started, open this entire project folder in your AI IDE, open the chat panel, and type:

Let's get started

Your AI will guide you through building your profile. It will ask for your real CV, LinkedIn profile, or other materials, and it will save this data locally in the profile/ folder. All data stays on your machine.

Step 2: Finding and Scoring Jobs

Once your profile is set up, you can ask the AI to find jobs for you. Try typing:

Find me jobs

The AI will search the web for real job postings in your target region, read the actual job descriptions, and score your fit (from 0 to 100) based on your real experience.

If you found a job yourself and want the AI to analyze it, paste the link and say:

Score this job: [Link]

Step 3: Managing Your Pipeline

The AI saves all the jobs it finds into a file called leads/scraped_leads.json.

  • Dashboard: Open dashboard/index.html (this page) and click Import. Select the scraped_leads.json file to see all your jobs visually in a Kanban board.
  • Excel: If you prefer spreadsheets, the AI automatically syncs new leads to excel/JobHunter_Pipeline.xlsx.

Step 4: Tailoring Your CV & Outreach

When you are ready to apply for a job that's in your pipeline, ask the AI to help you apply:

Tailor my CV for [Company Name]

The AI will rewrite your CV to highlight the experience most relevant to that specific job description, without ever inventing fake experience. It will output a genuinely ATS-safe Word document (.docx) into the output/cvs/ folder.

You can also ask for outreach materials:

Write outreach for [Company Name]

The AI will draft a highly tailored cover letter and LinkedIn direct messages for the hiring manager.

Summary of Commands

You can talk to the AI naturally, but here are the core commands it understands best:

  • Let's get started - Run the initial profile setup.
  • Find me jobs - Search the web for new leads.
  • Score this job [link/text] - Evaluate a specific job description.
  • Tailor my CV for [Company] - Generate an ATS-compliant Word document tailored to the role.
  • Write outreach for [Company] - Draft a cover letter and DMs.
